Land of History and Culture

Saudi Arabia is the heart of the Arab and Islamic world, possessing a unique historical and cultural richness that spans thousands of years. From the archaeological sites of Al-Ula to the skyscrapers of Riyadh, the Kingdom forms a magnificent tapestry of past and present.

Within the framework of the ambitious Vision 2030, the Kingdom is witnessing a comprehensive developmental renaissance aimed at enhancing the tourism sector and making the Kingdom a leading global destination.
