Land of History and Culture

Saudi Arabia is the heart of the Arab and Islamic world, possessing a unique historical and cultural richness that spans thousands of years. From the archaeological sites of Al-Ula to the skyscrapers of Riyadh, the Kingdom forms a magnificent tapestry of past and present.

Within the framework of the ambitious Vision 2030, the Kingdom is witnessing a comprehensive developmental renaissance aimed at enhancing the tourism sector and making the Kingdom a leading global destination. From the green mountains of Asir to the Empty Quarter desert, Saudi Arabia offers a unique experience for every visitor.

Rich Heritage & Authentic Traditions

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia possesses a rich cultural heritage that reflects its ancient history and regional diversity. From folk arts to traditional cuisine, from handicrafts to authentic customs and traditions, Saudi culture forms a rich tapestry of legacies passed down through generations.

The Kingdom is currently witnessing a great cultural renaissance within the framework of Vision 2030, with the opening of global museums, art festivals and cultural centers that highlight this rich heritage to the world, while preserving authenticity and national identity.
