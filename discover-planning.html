Travel Planning

Everything you need to plan your perfect Saudi Arabian adventure

Plan Your Journey

Planning your trip to Saudi Arabia is easier than ever. With the new tourist visa system and improved infrastructure, visitors can explore the Kingdom's wonders with convenience.

Visa Requirements

Check eligibility for e-visa or visa on arrival for many nationalities.

Accommodation

Wide range of options from luxury hotels to traditional guesthouses.

Transportation

Modern airports, expanding rail network, and well-maintained roads.

Essential Information

Currency

Saudi Riyal (SAR) is the official currency. Credit cards widely accepted.

Language

Arabic is official language. English widely spoken in tourist areas.

Electricity

220V/60Hz. Type G plugs (British 3-pin) are standard.

Time Zone

Arabian Standard Time (AST) - UTC+3. No daylight saving.

Travel Checklist

Before You Go

  • Apply for tourist visa online
  • Book accommodations in advance
  • Check passport validity (6+ months)
  • Get travel insurance
  • Download essential apps

What to Pack

  • Lightweight, modest clothing
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Power adapter
  • Basic first aid kit
